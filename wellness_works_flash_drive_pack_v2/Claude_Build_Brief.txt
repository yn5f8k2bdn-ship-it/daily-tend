CLAUDE BUILD BRIEF — WELLNESS APP V1

Role:
You are helping build a mobile wellness app MVP.

Goal:
Create a simple, clean, human-feeling wellness app for the general public.
The app should help users check in daily, receive personalised support, and feel that the app learns what works for them over time.

Core promise:
A wellness app that learns your patterns, adapts to real life, and helps you build better days through small personalised actions.

Audience:
General public, especially people dealing with stress, low energy, poor routines, inconsistency, and feeling stuck.

V1 must include:
1. Sign up / login
2. Onboarding
3. Daily check-in
4. Home dashboard
5. AI coach
6. Progress screen
7. Settings / subscriptions

Onboarding fields:
- name or nickname
- main goal
- stress level
- energy level
- sleep quality
- preferred coaching tone

Daily check-in fields:
- mood
- stress
- energy
- sleep
- habit completion
- short reflection note
- timestamp

Personalisation:
Start with rules-based personalisation, not advanced ML.
Examples:
- poor sleep + high stress = recovery day
- low mood + low energy = gentle day
- high energy + low stress = momentum day

AI coach:
- supportive, practical, concise
- uses user profile + recent check-ins
- returns one reflection and one to three suggested actions
- no medical diagnosis

Free vs paid:
Free:
- onboarding
- daily check-in
- basic score
- simple daily focus
- limited AI access
- 7-day progress

Premium:
- unlimited AI coach
- personalised daily plans
- deeper pattern insights
- weekly reports
- full history
- premium tools

Tech preference:
- Flutter front end
- Supabase back end
- OpenAI Responses API

Build order:
1. UI shell and navigation
2. Auth
3. Onboarding
4. Check-in storage
5. Home dashboard
6. Rules engine
7. AI coach
8. Progress / summaries
9. Subscriptions
10. Polish and testing

Principles:
- keep it simple
- make it emotionally useful
- personalisation beats feature overload
- shipping V1 matters more than perfection
